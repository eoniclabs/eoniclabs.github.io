About Us

Founded in Sweden, Eonic Labs designs, builds and operates software products that help businesses navigate the evolving digital advertising landscape. Our team brings deep expertise in full-stack engineering, cloud infrastructure, and data-driven product development.

We take ideas from concept to scalable product — combining technical leadership with hands-on execution to deliver platforms used by agencies, brands and creators across Europe.
